Meet our Team!

Candi

Candi has been the owner of Vanity Fur for 11 years, and has been grooming for 21 years. She started Vanity Fur in her home, until moving to our current location in December of 2023. Her favorite part about grooming is helping animals feel and look good.

Paris

Paris, the Shop Dog, is a 5 year old Lavender Merle Chihuahua, that loves people. She loves hanging out with and inspiring our team every day.

Hailee

Hailee, Candi's daughter, is always helping out all over the shop, bathing dogs, answering the phone, and greeting customers as our receptionist. She has been working at the family business for 8 years, and enjoys painting and drawing.

Hannah

Hannah has been grooming for two years, and enjoys being able to meet and connect with dogs and their owners. She has always loved dogs, and enjoys the opportunity to love on other people's pets. In her spare time she likes to do creative things like crochet, baking, and nail art.

Trinity

Trinity has loved animals since she grew up on a farm training horses and dogs. She has been grooming for 20 years, and grooming cats for 15 years. Her favorite part of her job is making animals look cute and feel comfortable.

Hailey

Hailey is the newest addition to the Vanity Fur Team. As a bather she loves helping your furry friends become squeaky clean. She loves meeting new dogs, and spending time with friends in the outdoors.
