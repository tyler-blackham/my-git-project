About Us

Vanity Fur started 11 years ago in Candi's home, where she and her family groomed dogs until 2023, when we moved into our current facility. One of the most important things to us is a high quality experience for our customers and their owners. We strive to have a clean and positive environment so that your pets can have an enjoyable experience.

Our goal is to treat your pets how you would treat them. We see them as part of your family, and aim to put safety and quality above all else. We want you to have a quality experience at Vanity Fur, and to get what you want out of each appointment, so that you and your pet can feel at home in our Salon, and go back home happy and healthy.
