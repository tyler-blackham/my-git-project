Pricing

At Vanity Fur, we do everything twice to ensure a quality haircut and cleaning for your furry friend.

We start by bringing your dog to the table where we will begin to cut and brush out their hair, trim or grind their nails, then clean and pluck their ears. From there it's to the bath, where we use a dirt stripping shampoo to get a nice, deep clean. Next, we use a lavender shampoo to to help your pet smell lovely. We then dry them fully with high powered dryer, and finally get them back on the grooming table for their finish clip.

Every groom comes with a bandana, bow, or bowtie

Do you have a Puppy?

Grooming can be a very stressful experience for dogs, especially puppies. They are not used to being touched, held, or having the clippers vibrate against their skin. Grooming can be a long process therefore, they need time and experience to adjust properly to a full groom.
